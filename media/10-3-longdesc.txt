The flowchart shows the network map of an informal organization with relationships existing between different members in the organization. The “C E O” is directly related to the “Assistant to the C E O” and the “V P of Sales.” The assistant to the C E O is directly related to the “Mail Clerk,” “Salesperson 2,” and “Assistant to the V P.” The mail clerk is linked to the “Custodial Worker.” The second salesperson and the assistant to the V P are directly related to the “V P of Sales.” The V P of sales is further related to the “Salesperson 1” and the “Customer Service Manager.” The first sales person and the customer service manager are shown further related to the “Customer Service Representative 1” in a loop.
