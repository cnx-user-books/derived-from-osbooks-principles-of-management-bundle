The flowchart shows five steps in the planning process. Step 1: Developing an awareness of the present state. Step 2: Establishing outcome statements which includes Goal planning, Domain planning, and Hybrid planning. Step 3: Premising which includes forecasting and formulating assumptions. Step 4: Determining a course of action which includes Identifying alternatives, Evaluating alternatives and Selecting alternatives. Step 5: Formulating supportive plans, Making changes in existing plans, and creating new supportive plans.
