The screenshot shows a sample of a Gannt chart for producing OpenStax Principles of Management Project prepared on a spreadsheet. It shows a list of project tasks, events, and milestones in column A. The start date and the end date assigned to each specific task appear in the columns from B to L of the second row. It also shows horizontal colored bars of different lengths marked below the scheduled dates, to represent the sequences, timing, and time span for each event.
