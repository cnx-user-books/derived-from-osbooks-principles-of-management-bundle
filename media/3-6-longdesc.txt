The diagram illustrates the different management techniques given by some of the famous management thinkers, thus contributing to the development of management thought. The management techniques given by each management thinker are shown in the diagram as follows: Elton Mayo: Teams and Motivation; Henri Fayol: Strategy and Organizational Design; Max Weber: Organizational Design; Frederick Winslow Taylor: Motivation and Human Resource Compensation Design; Chester Barnard and Mary Parker Follett: Leadership.
