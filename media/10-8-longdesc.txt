The diagram shows Lewin’s Change Model divided into three phases in which organizational changes occur. The phases are “Unfreeze,” “Move,” and “Refreeze.”  The characteristics of each phase are further listed as follows. Unfreeze: Examine status quo; Increase driving forces for change; Decrease resisting forces against. Move: Take action; Make changes; Involve people. Refreeze: Make change permanent; Establish new way of things; Reward desired outcomes.
